FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do you offer a free site visit?⌄
Yes — completely free, no obligation. We visit your site, take measurements and photos, then prepare a detailed quote. There's no charge for this service.
How long does a project typically take?⌄
Standard print jobs are ready in 48 hours. Signage fabrication takes 5–10 working days depending on complexity. Full retail fit-outs are typically 3–6 weeks from approval.
Do you install outside Delhi NCR?⌄
Yes. We have a PAN India installation network. We've handled multi-city rollouts for major retail brands across India. Speak to us about your locations.
What information should I share for a quote?⌄
Share your site photos, rough dimensions, the type of signage or print you need, and your deadline. The more detail the better — WhatsApp is the easiest way to share photos.
Do you handle design as well?⌄
Yes. We have an in-house design team that creates brand-aligned artwork for every project. If you have existing brand guidelines, we work within them. If not, we create from scratch.
Are there any hidden charges?⌄
Never. Our quotes are fully itemised — materials, fabrication, delivery and installation are all listed separately. What you see is what you pay.
